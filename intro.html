About Me

I am a final-year student pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science from Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology,Greater Noida,Uttar pradesh,India.Throughout my academic journey, I have acquired proficiency in a diverse set of programming languages and technologies, including Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and React. Additionally, I have honed my abilities through practical application, having completed a comprehensive course on Java Full Stack Development with Wipro and another on Python with Infosys. Additionally, I developed a React application named TodosList, which demonstrates my ability to create intuitive and efficient user interfaces while seamlessly integrating with backend systems.I am eager to contribute my skills and passion for software development to your team, collaborating on projects that push the boundaries of technology and drive meaningful impact.
